About This Project
SAP BTP Capstone Project — University Level
Problem Statement

NovaTech Industries Pvt. Ltd., a mid-size manufacturing company, suffers from a manual PO approval process that causes 3–11 day delays, 23% budget overruns due to poor cross-departmental visibility, and disconnected FI-MM workflows requiring manual reconciliation.

The goal is to build a cloud-native solution on SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP) that automates PO creation, multi-level approvals, and real-time budget tracking across FI, MM, and SD functional areas.

Key Features
  • Real-time PO creation with vendor & material master integration
  • Automated multi-level approval workflow with threshold-based routing
  • Live budget consumption tracking per cost center & GL account
  • FI-MM integration: three-way match for GRN & invoice verification
  • SD module: sales order lifecycle from inquiry to billing
  • SAP BTP Workflow Service for process orchestration
  • Role-based access control (Requester / Manager / Finance)
  • Email notifications via SAP Integration Suite
